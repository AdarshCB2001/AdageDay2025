Message from Managing Director

A reflection on 24 years of Adage’s journey

Welcome to Adage Day – our celebration of OTOS: One Team, One Soul.

Over the next two days, we celebrate the spirit that defines Adage – unity, passion, and the power of working together.

We have 6 dynamic teams, each led by a fantastic captain, ready to compete with full energy. Let’s run faster, think smarter, and play harder as we chase the trophy. Compete fiercely, give your best, and enjoy every moment.

But above all, when the competitions end, let’s come together and celebrate what truly makes Adage special — our togetherness, our bond, and our unwavering team spirit.

So fasten your seatbelts for two days of non-stop excitement, competition, and celebration.

Enjoy the Adage Day extravaganza!
