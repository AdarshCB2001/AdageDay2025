Event Guidelines

Follow the instructions to ensure smooth event experience

check_circle

DO's

  • Report on time for all events and follow the schedule strictly.
  • Dress as per the event-specific dress code (Sports / Photoshoot / Awards / Gala).
  • Do remember your standing position for the photoshoot.
  • Supervise your children and make sure they stay within designated areas.
  • Enjoy responsibly, stay involved, and encourage others in all activities.
block

DON'Ts

  • Do not litter or throw food, tissue, or bottles around — use dustbins provided.
  • Do not damage property, décor, lighting, or stage arrangements.
  • Children should not run, loiter, or interrupt any performances.
  • Do not wander into restricted areas, backstage zones, or performance zones.
  • Children below 3 years are not allowed at the event venues.
